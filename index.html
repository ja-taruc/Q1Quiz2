Summary

    The Story of Toy Story 2 is a sequel to it's first part on living toys such as Woody, Buzz Lightyear, Jessie, and it's owner Andy. After Woody gets stolen because Al McWhiggin brings him to his penthouse and tries to sell him to a toy museum abroad, where Woody meets the Roundup Gang such as Jessie, Bullseye, and Stinky Pete. His friends try to save him but Stinky Pete the Prospector doesn't allow them to leave the warehouse.

    Jessie voiced by Mary Kay Bergman, she is the sister like character of Woody and his bestfriend while Buzz Lightyear is understood to be her a little more than a friend and is a neutral person within Woody and Buzz Lightyear.
